Chinese Classical Dance

I have studied Chinese classical dance and folk dance for several years, been a choreographer and actor in school activities.
Through experience of organizing and preparing some dance special performances, I love the feeling of overcoming challenge via cooperation.
Recently, I’m obsessing with history of Chinese dance, became a member of Han dynasty portrait dance reproduction project (2018-). It is significant work to the innovation and inheritance of Chinese dance, I’m proud of what I do.
